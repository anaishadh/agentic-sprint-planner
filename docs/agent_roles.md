## Agent Roles

### Business Analyst Agent
Understands the problem statement and converts high-level requirements into clear business goals and constraints.

### Task Breakdown Agent
Breaks business goals into actionable technical tasks that can be executed within a sprint.

### Sprint Planner Agent
Organizes tasks into a sprint based on priorities, dependencies, and sprint length.

### Allocation Agent
Assigns tasks to team members based on capacity, skill level, and availability.
